- **TAM (Total Addressable Market):** $15B global story-driven games market

- **SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market):** $35M realistic capture within first 3 years based on genre performance of comparable indie/AA titles (Outer Wilds, FTL, Into the Breach, Citizen Sleeper)
