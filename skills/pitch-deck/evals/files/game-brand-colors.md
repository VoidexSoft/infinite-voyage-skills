## Logo Description
The Infinite Voyage logo features a stylized infinity symbol (lemniscate) integrated with a spaceship silhouette. The ship forms the central crossing point of the infinity loop, suggesting endless exploration. The left loop contains a subtle star cluster, and the right loop contains a planetary ring.

- **Primary version:** Nebula Blue on Deep Space background
- **Reversed version:** Cosmos White on Deep Space background
- **Monochrome version:** Single-color (white or black) for constrained contexts
- **Minimum size:** 120px wide for digital, 1 inch wide for print
- **Clear space:** Maintain at least 0.5x logo height on all sides

## Image Guidance
- **Style:** Semi-realistic digital painting with high contrast and dramatic lighting
- **Mood:** Mysterious, vast, awe-inspiring — convey the feeling of standing on the edge of the unknown
- **Composition:** Use strong diagonals and leading lines to guide the eye; horizon lines at 1/3 or 2/3 position
- **Color treatment:** Images should lean toward the blue-orange complementary palette; desaturate competing hues
- **Concept art subjects:** Ancient alien ruins, ship interiors, planet surfaces, star fields, alien flora
- **Avoid:** Cartoonish styles, overly dark/unreadable images, generic stock sci-fi, cluttered compositions
